Disaster Management for Uttarakhand

Be disaster ready with real-time alerts, shelter info, and offline support.

About the Project

Helping Hand Uttarakhand is inspired by the Smart India Hackathon 2018 winning solution for Assam. It aims to provide forecasting, safe evacuation, volunteer coordination, and government monitoring in both online and offline modes during disasters like floods and landslides.

Key Features

🌦️ Forecasting Alerts
🏠 Shelter Locator
🚌 Transport Tracking
🤝 Volunteer Platform
📊 Govt Dashboard
📡 Offline Services (SMS, IVRS)

Modules

Users

Access shelters, safety tips, and real-time updates.

Volunteers

Register shelters, provide support, and assist during disasters.

Government

Monitor shelters, resources, and disaster situations with dashboards.
